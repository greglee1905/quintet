About Us

Our Time Wind Quintet invites you in.

Through intentional and purposeful programming, Our Time offers a moment for attendees to reconnect with their simple but profound capacity to listen and hold space for each other.

The group aspires to share their joyous spirit through performance, educational engagement, and community partnerships. Our Time opens dialogues to weave together a more supportive humanity.
